Your deck is an extension of your home

a place where you can relax, entertain, and enjoy the outdoors. At Northwest BNR, we understand the importance of having a beautiful and functional deck that complements your lifestyle and adds value to your property. Our team of skilled professionals specializes in deck design, construction, and maintenance, and we pride ourselves on delivering exceptional service and craftsmanship that exceeds your expectations.

Whether you're looking to build a brand new deck or upgrade your existing one, we have the expertise and resources to bring your vision to life. We use only the finest materials and cutting-edge techniques to ensure that your deck is built to last and withstand the Pacific Northwest's harsh weather conditions. From classic wood decks to modern composite decks, we offer a wide range of options to suit your taste and budget.
